Why Does This Page Exist?


Detroit and its surroundings has an ever-growing community of black owned and led reformer and mat Pilates practicioners. The intention of this guide is to grow this community and celebrate black entreprenuership and wellness. Please use this guide with care and feel free to contribute in any way you feel led.
